Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 5, 2025

Welcome to DKWIN. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Info: Name, email, phone number, country, etc.
  • Transaction Data: Deposits, withdrawals, and betting history.
  • Device Info: IP address, browser type, device ID.
  • Cookies: Used for personalization, analytics, and ad targeting.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To process payments and withdrawals.
  • To improve the platform and offer tailored content.
  • To detect and prevent fraud and misuse.
  • For customer support and communication.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your data. We only share it with:

  • Trusted payment processors
  • Analytics and security tools
  • Legal authorities if required by law

4. Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your account data.
  • Opt-out of marketing emails.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

5. Data Security

We use encryption, firewalls, and regular security audits to protect your information.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to improve your experience. You can modify your browser settings to control cookies.

7. Age Restriction

DKWIN is intended for users aged 18 years and above. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, contact: privacy@dkwin.com
